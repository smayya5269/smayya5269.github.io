Ghosts - Introduction

August 3, 2020 in Ghosts

Hi again and welcome back to day 2 of this blog! After some thinking, I decided to start off with something I’ve longed to write about for quite a long time now. So let’s hop in and start reading!

Let’s start off with a bit of info about me! I play Roblox, an online gaming platform and I highly enjoy it. Call me weird or cheezy but hey! It’s what I enjoy!

Anyways, my first story I’m focusing on is called Ghosts. I guess you could call ghosts some spiritual thing of life but honestly, after getting the game in touch and dying in Roblox a million times, the whole concept of ghosts wasnt so bad cause I always knew you could spectate and respawn and stuff. In real life, if you have passed away, you just move on. Never know how to improve, and never remembering the happy and sad times. Completely different from Roblox.

This story is viewed through the perspective of two characters who face challenges. The person completing this in real life must complete all 5 stages of the challenge and then the gamer who has completed this before must react to the completion of each stage.

Well, thats the intro to my story, Ghosts! Chapter one comes out tomorrow! Hope you enjoy!

-Sneha 8/3/2020
