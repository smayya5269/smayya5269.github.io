Chapter 1: Terrified

August 4, 2020 in Ghosts

“Welcome to Real-imination Tower, here is lovely Los Angeles, California! Real-imination Tower is based off of a game from the famous one and only platform for gaming- Roblox!! I will be the main host for today!” host Duce Brapples exclaimed into his mic.

“Today’s competitor is put to the test to complete 5 dangerous stages in the tower and be examined by a pro at this tower, a gamer who has played this before and has died multiple times and respawned! Hey if anything happens, at least our competitor signed the waiver! The contestant was a random pick from people all over the world! If she successfully lives and still completes it with a good note from the gamer, she wins 100,000 dollars! That’s enough from me, now I will pass the mic on to our co-host, Merry ‘Mass! The show’s all yours, Merry!” Duce Brapples pointed to a young woman with heavy makeup who looked like she was in her late 20’s.

“Thanks Duce! Our challenger today is Odette Austin! Lets ask her a few questions before we begin with the challenges!” Merry ‘Mass reported.

Merry ‘Mass: “Hello Odette! You are the winner of the random contestant raffle! Tell us a little about yourself, like where you are from!”

Odette: “Ummmmm….Hi? M-my name is Odette, im 16 and… uhhhhhh I come from Cheyenna, Wisconsin.”

Merry ‘Mas: “Amazing! You come from the Cheese state! That must rock! Anyways, why did you want to perform here?”

Odette: “Well, I heard you guys were doing a raffle with a lot of people in the world on T.V. and I was really bored so I just opted in… I never imagined that I would get selected. I just came for fun.”

Merry ‘Mass: “That’s so cool! I can assure one thing for sure though, you sure aren’t going to be bored now! Final question and most important of all- Are you nervous?”

Odette: “Honestly, I just found out that I’m being compared to a pro gamer who completed this, and there is 100,000 dollars on the line so I would be lying if I said I wasn’t nervous. I’m definetely nervous. I’m not exactly the sporty type so yeah…Did I mention I came here for fun? I’d like it if I didn’t get injured if I may say so myself.”

“Well, folks you heard it from our contestant today! Let’s get started! Duce, please introduce her first challenge!” Merry ‘Mass announced excitedly.

“Sure thing, Merry! The challenges will get harder and harder! The first task- You must jump from thin block to another across water. In the water are mild leeches. Don’t worry, you wont get too hurt… Well I hope not,” Duce Brapples explained.

Odette gulped.
